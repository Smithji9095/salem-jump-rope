Achievement Award

The Ace Honor Roll

A.C.E. stands for: Achieves Hard Skills, Chooses Character, Example to Others. It is Salem Jump Rope's highest honor for athletes.

What is an Ace?

An ACE is a student-athlete who has mastered Salem Jump Rope skill and character development levels, demonstrated Christlike leadership in practice and mentorship, completed the Humility Project, and been recognized by the coaching staff for dedication, character, and excellence.

Our Foundation

The Salem Jump Rope Story

Salem Jump Rope grew from years of physical education teaching, school jump rope teams, driveway practice with family, and small community clubs. The program carries forward the belief that jump rope can shape confidence, character, leadership, and faith.

Coach Tiffany Carroll's journey began in 2008 after graduating from East Carolina University and learning from strong school and jump rope mentors. That foundation eventually led to COA SKIP ITS in 2024 and the launch of Salem Jump Rope in 2026.

Today, Salem Jump Rope is being built as a performance based team where students learn to work hard, serve others, mentor younger athletes, and leave a lasting impact.

Team Culture

Mentorship Program

Because mentorship matters: experienced athletes help younger teammates grow in skill, character, confidence, and belonging.

The Foundation of Our Culture

At Salem Jump Rope, some of the most valuable lessons learned through sports happen through relationships. Mentorship is a vital part of the team culture because it helps every athlete feel valued, supported, and known.

How It Works

Experienced team members who have been on the team for at least one year may have the opportunity to become mentors. Coaches evaluate students based on skill readiness, character, responsibility, and leadership before recommending them for this role.

Mentors are paired with younger teammates and provide one on one skill support, encouragement, and guidance at practices. Beyond jump rope, they model positive character and help create a welcoming team environment.

Who Can Become a Mentor?

  • Athletes who have been on the team at least one year
  • Recommended completion through Level 3 skills
  • Demonstrated character that reflects team values
  • Nominated and approved by the coaching staff
  • Committed to mentor training, accountability, and high standards of conduct
📸

Questions About Mentorship?

Jump Rope for Life

Fitness & Conditioning

Jump rope is one of the most effective, accessible, and fun fitness tools available to everyone.

Why Jump Rope?

❤️

Cardiovascular Health

Jumping rope elevates heart rate quickly, improving cardiovascular endurance with less joint impact than running.

⚡

Agility & Coordination

Regular rope jumping builds foot speed, rhythm, balance, and full body coordination at every age.

🔥

Calorie Burn

Jump rope can burn up to 10 to 16 calories per minute, which is more than many common cardio exercises.

🧠

Mental Focus

The rhythm and timing of jump rope train concentration and mental discipline that carries into school and life.

💰

Affordable

All you need is a rope. No gym membership, no equipment, no commute. Jump anywhere, anytime.

👨‍👩‍👧

Family Friendly

Jump rope is one of the few activities the whole family can do together, from toddlers to grandparents.
